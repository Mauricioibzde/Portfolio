Let me tell you a little about myself!

Since I started getting interested in technology, I’ve always enjoyed understanding how things work — both in hardware and software. This curiosity led me to learn about electronic device maintenance and to gain knowledge in system integration, which gave me a broader view of how different technologies connect and work together. Although I enjoy the more technical side, I discovered that my true passion is software development. That’s where I can combine logic, creativity, and constant learning. I’m currently taking the Fullstack course at Rocketseat, aiming to become a complete developer. Throughout the course, I’ve learned to modularize projects, create servers, use packages, and develop practical applications. I have a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m currently deepening my knowledge in Node.js, building APIs and integrating applications with databases. Additionally, I have skills in graphic design, especially with Adobe Photoshop, which helps me create more attractive and functional interfaces. I enjoy balancing the visual and technical aspects of projects to deliver complete and well-structured results. I use GitHub to version my projects and track progress, always following good commit practices and maintaining clean, organized code. I currently live in Munich, Germany, seeking an Ausbildung as a Fachinformatiker für Anwendungsentwicklung. My goal is to keep learning, gaining hands-on experience, and building a solid career in software development. I’m curious, dedicated, and passionate about technology — seeing every challenge as an opportunity to grow and turn ideas into real solutions.
